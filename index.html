Chapter 1
🌅
The Great Morning Chase

Every single morning, the cat wakes up, stretches dramatically, and announces:"TODAY IS THE DAY I CATCH THE RACCOON."

The raccoon, eating stolen cereal on the roof, hears this, looks directly at the cat, and waves. Then eats another spoonful. The cat trips over its own tail running outside. The raccoon is already gone.🥄 Gone. With the cereal. Again.

Chapter 2
🪤
Operation Sneaky Trap

The cat spent 3 hours building an elaborate trap: a cardboard box, a stick, a string, and a piece of cheese as bait.

The raccoon walked up, read the hand-written sign that said "DEFINITELY NOT A TRAP," took the cheese, left a tiny thank-you note, and disappeared into the bushes. The cat was somehow still surprised.📝 The note said: "5 stars, very generous." 🦝

Chapter 3
🚀
The Speed Problem

Scientists have studied the raccoon's escape speed and concluded it is "technically impossible."

The cat can run at full speed, turn a corner, and somehow the raccoon is already sitting there eating a sandwich, looking bored. The cat once chased the raccoon around a tree for 45 minutes. The raccoon had been sitting at home watching TV for 44 of those minutes.🥪 Nobody knows how. Not even the raccoon.

Chapter 4
🤝
The Peace Treaty (That Lasted 4 Minutes)

One day the cat proposed a truce. They shook hands. They agreed: no more chasing, no more stealing snacks. They even took a photo together.

Four minutes later, the raccoon stole the cat's tuna sandwich right out of its paw, pointed finger guns, winked, and backflipped over the fence.📸 The photo is still on the fridge. Mocking the cat daily.

Chapter 5
💙
The Twist Nobody Expected

Here's the thing though — the cat never actually wanted to catch the raccoon. It just liked having somewhere to run to every morning.

And the raccoon? It always circled back. Left snacks on the doorstep. Saved a seat. Made sure the cat was never really alone.

Some chases aren't about catching. They're about always finding a way back to each other. 🐱🦝💙 Even if one of them keeps stealing the cereal.

The Night It All Changed

One evening the raccoon didn't run. Just… sat down next to the cat. The cat, confused, also sat. They watched the sky together in silence.

The Rainy Day Agreement

On a rainy Tuesday, the raccoon showed up at the cat's door with no explanation, two blankets, and a bag of popcorn.

The cat opened the door without even asking why. They watched movies all day. The raccoon fell asleep on the cat's shoulder. The cat didn't move for three hours so they wouldn't wake up.🍿 Arm completely numb. Absolutely worth it.

"I think you're my favourite thing."

The cat kept every single one.💌 There's a whole drawer. Don't open it or they'll both cry.
